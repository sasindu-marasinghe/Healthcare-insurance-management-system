DAWASA Healthcare Insurance Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility:

  • You must meet the eligibility criteria specified by Dawasa Healthcare Insurance Company to enroll in our healthcare insurance services.
  • You may be required to provide accurate and up-to-date personal and medical information during the enrollment process.

2. Coverage:

  • The coverage provided by Dawsa Healthcare Insurance Company is subject to the terms and conditions outlined in your insurance policy.
  • Coverage options, limitations, exclusions, and benefits will be clearly stated in your policy documents.
  • It is your responsibility to review and understand the coverage details and any restrictions or limitations that may apply.

3. Premiums and Payments:

  • Premiums for healthcare insurance coverage must be paid according to the payment schedule specified in your policy.
  • Failure to pay premiums on time may result in the termination or suspension of your coverage.
  • Premium amounts may be subject to change, and you will be notified in advance of any changes.

4. Claims:

  • Claims for healthcare services covered under your policy should be submitted in accordance with the guidelines provided by Dawsa Healthcare Insurance Company.
  • Dawsa Healthcare Insurance Company will assess and process claims based on the terms and conditions outlined in your policy.
  • You may be required to provide supporting documentation and evidence for claims.

5. Network of Providers:

  • Dawsa Healthcare Insurance Company may have a network of healthcare providers with whom they have agreements for preferred rates or direct billing.
  • The availability of specific healthcare providers may vary depending on your location and the network of providers in your area.

6. Policy Changes and Cancellations:

  • Changes to your policy, such as adding or removing coverage options, may be allowed subject to the terms and conditions outlined by Dawsa Healthcare Insurance Company.
  • You may have the right to cancel your policy within a specified cancellation period, as stated in your policy documents.

7. Privacy and Confidentiality:

  • Dawsa Healthcare Insurance Company will handle your personal and medical information in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.
